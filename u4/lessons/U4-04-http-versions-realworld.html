HTTP Versions — Lanes on a Highway

A single-lane road forces cars to go one behind another — one breakdown holds up everyone. Add lanes and traffic flows side by side. Make the lanes fully independent and one stalled car no longer blocks the others. That's the journey from HTTP/1 to /2 to /3.
