Dead Letter Queue — The Returns Bin

At a sorting office, most parcels get delivered — but some have a smudged or wrong address and keep coming back. Re-attempting one forever jams the whole line. So it's set aside in a 'returns' bin: the good parcels keep moving, and a clerk sorts out the problem ones later.
