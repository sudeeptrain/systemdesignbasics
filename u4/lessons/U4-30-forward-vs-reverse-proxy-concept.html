Forward vs Reverse Proxy

Both proxies stand in the middle, but on opposite ends. A forward proxy sits in front of clients and represents them to the internet (hiding who they are). A reverse proxy sits in front of servers and represents them to the world (hiding how many there are, adding LB, caching, TLS).
