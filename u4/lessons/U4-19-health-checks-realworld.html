Health Checks — A Shift Manager's Roll-Call

A shift manager doesn't assign customers to a cashier who's gone home sick. They do a regular roll-call: anyone not responding is taken off the rota; when they're back and well, they're quietly put back on. A load balancer health-checks its servers exactly this way.
