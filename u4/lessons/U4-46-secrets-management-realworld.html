Secrets Management — A Bank Safe-Deposit Box

Leaving your spare key under the doormat is how break-ins happen. A bank safe-deposit box is the opposite: valuables kept in one guarded vault, retrieved only when needed, with a log of every access — and if a key is compromised, the bank re-keys the lock. That's secrets management.
