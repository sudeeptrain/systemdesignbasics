QUIC

QUIC is the transport behind HTTP/3. Built on UDP, it folds the connection and TLS setup into one fast step (often 0-RTT on repeat visits), avoids TCP's head-of-line blocking, and can survive a network switch — your connection lives on as you move from Wi-Fi to cellular.
