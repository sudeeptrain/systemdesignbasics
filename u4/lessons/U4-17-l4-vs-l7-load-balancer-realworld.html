L4 vs L7 — Sorting Mail by Zip vs by Reading It

One mail sorter just reads the zip code on the outside and flings each letter to a region — blazing fast, but it never knows what's inside. Another opens and reads each letter to route it to exactly the right department. L4 is the first sorter; L7 is the second.
