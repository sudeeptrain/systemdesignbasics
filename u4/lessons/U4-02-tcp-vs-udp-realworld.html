TCP vs UDP — Registered Post vs Postcards

Registered post is like TCP: every item is signed for, lost ones are re-sent, and they arrive in order — reliable, but slower. Tossing out postcards is like UDP: fast and cheap, but no tracking and some never arrive. You pick by what you can afford to lose.
