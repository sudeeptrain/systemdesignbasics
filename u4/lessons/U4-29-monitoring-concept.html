Monitoring (Prometheus & Grafana)

You can't fix what you can't see. Services expose metrics; Prometheus scrapes them on a schedule and stores the time-series; Grafana turns them into dashboards. When a metric crosses a threshold, an alert fires — so you hear about trouble before your users do.
