Global Load Balancing — Your Nearest Branch

A pizza chain delivers from the branch closest to you — faster, and it spreads orders across shops. If your local branch shuts for the night, your order is automatically handled by the next-nearest one: a little slower, but you still get fed. That's global load balancing.
