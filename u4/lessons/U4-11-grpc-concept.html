gRPC

gRPC is built for service-to-service calls. Instead of verbose JSON over HTTP/1, it sends compact binary (Protocol Buffers) over HTTP/2, with a strongly-typed contract both sides share. The result: smaller payloads, faster calls, and built-in streaming.
