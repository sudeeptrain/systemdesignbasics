mTLS — Two Spies Trading Passwords

At a normal shop, the shopkeeper shows credentials but you, the customer, stay anonymous. Two spies meeting are different: BOTH must give the secret password — and anyone who can't is turned away. mTLS is that two-way check, ideal for locking down service-to-service traffic.
