WAF (Web Application Firewall)

A network firewall filters by IP and port; a WAF works one layer higher — it reads the actual HTTP request. It inspects URLs, headers, and bodies for attack patterns (SQL injection, XSS, bad bots) and blocks malicious requests before they reach your app.
