Sticky vs Stateless — Your Regular Barista

If only your usual barista remembers your order, you have to wait for them — and if they're off sick, your 'usual' is forgotten. Write the order on a loyalty card you carry instead, and any barista can serve you. Sticky pins you to one; stateless lets anyone help.
