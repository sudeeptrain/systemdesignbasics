Rate Limiting — A Nightclub Bouncer

A club has a safe capacity. The bouncer lets people in until it's full, then holds the line — "sorry, wait." As people leave, slots free up and the queue moves again. A token bucket rate limiter is exactly that bouncer, counting how many can enter right now.
