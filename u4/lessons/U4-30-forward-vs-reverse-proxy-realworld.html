Forward vs Reverse Proxy — Personal Shopper vs Receptionist

A personal shopper goes out to the shops on your behalf — the shops only ever see the shopper, not you. A company receptionist faces the public and quietly routes callers to whichever staff member is free — callers never see the team behind. Same middleman idea, opposite sides.
