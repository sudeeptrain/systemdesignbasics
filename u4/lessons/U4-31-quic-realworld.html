QUIC — A Courier Who Knows You, Not Your Address

A regular courier ties deliveries to your street address — move house mid-order and it's lost. A courier who recognizes YOU just keeps delivering wherever you've gone. QUIC works that way: it identifies the connection, not the network, so it survives you switching from Wi-Fi to cellular.
