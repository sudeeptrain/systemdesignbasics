DDoS Attacks & Mitigation

A DDoS attack floods your server with junk traffic from thousands of machines, drowning out real users. Mitigation puts a scrubbing layer in front — a CDN, rate limits, traffic filtering — that absorbs and discards the bad traffic while letting legitimate requests through.
