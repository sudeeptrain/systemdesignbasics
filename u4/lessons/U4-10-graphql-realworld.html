GraphQL — À La Carte vs a Set Plate

A set plate comes fixed: you wanted the rice and curry but it arrives loaded with sides you'll leave untouched — and if you also fancy dessert, that's a second trip. À la carte lets you name exactly the dishes you want and get just those, in one order. GraphQL is à la carte.
