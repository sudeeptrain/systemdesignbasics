Deployment Strategies — Refreshing a Restaurant's Menu

Launching a new menu without closing the restaurant takes a plan. Update tables a few at a time (rolling); set up a whole second dining room and switch everyone at once (blue-green); or trial it on one table first and watch before rolling out to all (canary).
