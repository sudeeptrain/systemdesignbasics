API Pagination (offset vs cursor)

You don't return a million rows at once — you paginate. Offset pagination says "skip 20, give me the next 10" (simple, but slow on deep pages and breaks when rows are inserted). Cursor pagination says "give me what's after this item" (stable and fast).
