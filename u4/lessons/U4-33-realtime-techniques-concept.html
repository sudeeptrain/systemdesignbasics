Short Poll · Long Poll · SSE · WebSocket

Four ways to get fresh data to a browser. Short polling asks repeatedly. Long polling holds the request open until there's news. SSE is a one-way server→client stream. WebSocket is a full two-way pipe. Each trades simplicity for efficiency and power.
