SSE — A Live Radio Broadcast

Tune your radio to a station and you just listen — the broadcaster talks, you receive, one direction only. Lose the signal in a tunnel and the radio re-tunes itself when you come out. SSE is that broadcast: the server streams updates and the browser simply listens.
