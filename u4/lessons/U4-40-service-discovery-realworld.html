Service Discovery — A Taxi Dispatch Board

Riders don't memorize drivers' phone numbers — those change constantly. They call dispatch. Drivers check in as available, dispatch keeps a live board, and when a rider calls it sends a free, nearby driver. Service discovery is that dispatch board for your services.
