WAF — A Bouncer Who Checks Your Bag

A gate guard checks if you're on the list. A venue bouncer goes further — they look inside your bag for anything dangerous before letting you in. A WAF is that bag-checking bouncer: it reads the actual contents of each request and blocks the harmful ones before they reach your app.
