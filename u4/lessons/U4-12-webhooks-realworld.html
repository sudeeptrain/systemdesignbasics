Webhooks — A Doorbell, Not a Peephole

Waiting for a delivery by checking the peephole every minute is exhausting and you still miss the moment. A doorbell flips it: you do nothing until the courier presses it, then you know instantly. A webhook is that doorbell — the other service calls you when something happens.
