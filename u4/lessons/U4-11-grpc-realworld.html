gRPC — Two Pros Talking in Shorthand

Explaining everything in full plain sentences to a stranger is clear but slow. Two veterans who share a glossary fire off clipped shorthand — half the words, twice the speed — because both already agree what each term means. gRPC is that shorthand between your own services.
