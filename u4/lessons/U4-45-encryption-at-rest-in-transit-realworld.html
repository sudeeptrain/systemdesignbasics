Encryption — A Sealed Envelope and a Locked Diary

A secret is exposed in two places: while it's being carried (a postcard anyone can read) and while it's stored (a diary left open on a desk). Seal it in an envelope for the journey, and lock the diary at home. You need both — a nosy courier and a burglar are different threats.
