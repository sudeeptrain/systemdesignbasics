Pagination — Page Numbers vs a Bookmark

Find your place in a book by page number and someone inserts pages — your "page 30" now lands somewhere else, and you re-read a bit. Use a bookmark tucked after a specific paragraph and it always points to the right spot, no matter what's added. Offset is the page number; cursor is the bookmark.
