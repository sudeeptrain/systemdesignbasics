Hexagonal — A Universal Power Socket

A laptop doesn't care which country's wall socket it's in. It exposes a standard plug; a cheap adapter bridges to whatever's on the wall. Swap the adapter — UK, EU, US — and the laptop runs unchanged. That's ports and adapters.
