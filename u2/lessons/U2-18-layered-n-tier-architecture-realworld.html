N-Tier — Waiter, Kitchen, Pantry

A restaurant runs in layers. The waiter takes your order but never cooks; the kitchen cooks but doesn't grow the vegetables; the pantry stores ingredients. Each layer talks only to the next — and you can change the pantry without retraining a waiter.
