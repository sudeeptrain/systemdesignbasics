Pub/Sub vs Streaming — Live TV vs DVR

Old broadcast TV is pub/sub: the show airs once and if you're not watching, you miss it forever. A DVR or streaming service is event streaming: every episode is recorded to a library you can pause, rewind, and binge whenever you like.
