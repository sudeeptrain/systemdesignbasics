Cache Stampede — The Rush When the Sign Comes Down

A shop posts the day's special on a board so nobody has to ask. The instant that board is taken down to update it, every waiting customer rushes the one clerk at once to ask the price — overwhelming them. The fix: let one person ask while the rest wait for the new board.
