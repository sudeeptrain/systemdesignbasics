IPC — How Two Neighbors Share

Two households can't see into each other's homes. To cooperate they pass notes through the mail slot — safe, but each note is copied and handed over. Or they share a fence-side notice board both can write on directly — instant, but they must take turns to avoid scribbling over each other. That's IPC.
