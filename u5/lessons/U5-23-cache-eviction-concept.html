Cache Eviction (LRU / LFU)

A cache has limited space, so when it's full and a new item arrives, something must go. An eviction policy decides which: LRU drops the least-recently-used entry; LFU drops the least-frequently-used. The goal is to throw out what you're least likely to need again.
