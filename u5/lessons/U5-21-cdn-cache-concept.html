CDN Cache

A CDN caches static content (images, video, JS) on edge servers near users. The first request for an asset misses and fetches from the origin; after that, nearby users are served from the edge until the content's TTL expires and it's revalidated. Closer copies mean faster loads and far less origin traffic.
