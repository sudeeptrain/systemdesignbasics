Paging — A Coat-Check with Numbered Cubbies

At a coat check, your items don't go in one big reserved area. Each is put in whatever cubby is free, and a ticket stub records which stub maps to which cubby. Your stuff sits scattered, but the ticket finds it instantly — and no awkward gaps go to waste. That's paging.
