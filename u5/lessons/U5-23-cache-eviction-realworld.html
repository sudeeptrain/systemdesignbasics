Cache Eviction — Making Room on a Full Shelf

Your bookshelf is full and you've got a new book. Something has to come off. Which? By recency — donate the one you haven't opened in longest (LRU). Or by frequency — donate the one you've read fewest times (LFU). The aim is to part with what you're least likely to want again.
