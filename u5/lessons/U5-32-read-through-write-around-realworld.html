Read-Through & Write-Around — A Librarian Who Fetches

Read-through: you ask the librarian at the front desk; if the book isn't on the display shelf, the librarian fetches it from the stacks for you and shelves it — you never go yourself. Write-around: newly donated books go straight to the stacks, not the display, so the front shelf stays full of what people actually borrow.
