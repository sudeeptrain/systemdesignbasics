Segmentation — A House Divided by Room

A house isn't one big blind space — it's divided by purpose into rooms: kitchen, bedroom, garage, study. Each room has a location and a size, and you can't walk through a wall into the next one. Segmentation organizes a program's memory the same way, with a bounds check at every wall.
