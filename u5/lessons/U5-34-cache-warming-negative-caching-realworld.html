Cache Warming & Negative Caching — Prep Before the Rush

A smart café preps its popular dishes before the lunch rush, so the first customers are served instantly instead of waiting on a cold kitchen. And it posts a 'we don't do gluten-free' sign so staff stop running to the kitchen to ask the same dead-end question. Both turn predictable waits into instant answers.
