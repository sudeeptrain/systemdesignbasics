Deadlock — Two Cars at a One-Lane Bridge

Two cars enter a one-lane bridge from opposite ends. Each has taken half, each needs the half the other is on, and neither will reverse. Both are stuck forever. A deadlock needs four conditions together — break any one (say, agree who always yields) and it can't happen.
