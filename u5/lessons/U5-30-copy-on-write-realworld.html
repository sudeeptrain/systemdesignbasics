Copy-on-Write — Photocopy Only What You'll Mark Up

Two editors share one master report. They both just read most pages — no need to copy anything. The moment one wants to scribble edits on a page, they photocopy that single page and mark up their own copy. You only spend on copies for the pages that actually change.
