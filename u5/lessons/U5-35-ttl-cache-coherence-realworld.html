TTL & Coherence — Expiry Dates and Synced Branch Menus

An expiry date caps how long you trust the milk: a short date means fresh but frequent shopping; a long one means fewer trips but riskier. And when head office changes a price, every branch's menu must be updated — or customers at different shops get charged different amounts. That's TTL and coherence.
