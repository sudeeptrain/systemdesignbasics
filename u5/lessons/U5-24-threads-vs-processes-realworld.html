Threads vs Processes — Shared Office vs Separate Companies

Coworkers in one open office share the same whiteboard and files — they coordinate instantly, but can scribble over each other's notes. Separate companies each keep their own private offices — safe and independent, but they can only communicate by formally sending things across. Threads vs processes.
