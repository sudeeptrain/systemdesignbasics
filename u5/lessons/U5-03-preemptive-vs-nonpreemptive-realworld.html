Preemptive vs Non-Preemptive — The Supermarket Checkout

At a non-express till, whoever's at the front keeps the cashier until their whole trolley is rung up — one huge shop makes everyone behind wait. An express lane that pauses to wave through people with a few items keeps quick shoppers moving. That's preemptive vs non-preemptive scheduling.
