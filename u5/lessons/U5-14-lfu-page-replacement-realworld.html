LFU Replacement — Keep the Tools You Reach For Most

A small toolbox can only hold a few tools. LFU's rule: when you need room, drop whichever tool you've reached for the fewest times. It keeps your most-used tools handy — though it can stubbornly hang onto something you used heavily once and never touch again.
