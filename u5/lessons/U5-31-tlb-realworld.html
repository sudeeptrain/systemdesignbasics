TLB — A Speed-Dial Note vs the Phone Book

Every call needs a number, and looking each one up in the fat phone book is slow. So you keep a little speed-dial note of the numbers you call most. A name on the note? Dial instantly. Not on it? Look it up in the book — then add it to the note. The TLB is that speed-dial note for address lookups.
