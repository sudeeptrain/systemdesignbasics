Semaphore vs Mutex vs Spinlock — Getting a Desk

Three ways to manage shared desks. Mutex: one desk with a key — if taken, you go sit and wait to be called. Semaphore: a room with N desks — up to N people work at once. Spinlock: one prized desk you hover right beside, constantly re-checking — great if it frees in seconds, wasteful if not.
