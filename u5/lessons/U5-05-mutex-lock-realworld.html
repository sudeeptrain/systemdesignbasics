Mutex / Lock — The Single Restroom Key

A café has one restroom and one key behind the counter. To go in, you take the key; while you have it, nobody else can enter. They simply wait for the key to come back. That single key turns a potential scramble into an orderly one-at-a-time queue — exactly what a mutex does.
