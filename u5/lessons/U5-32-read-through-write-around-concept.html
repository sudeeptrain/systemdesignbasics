Read-Through & Write-Around Cache

Read-through: the cache itself loads from the database on a miss — the app just asks the cache, which handles the fetch. Write-around: writes go straight to the database, bypassing the cache, so write-only data doesn't evict hot read data. They pair well for read-heavy, write-rarely-read workloads.
