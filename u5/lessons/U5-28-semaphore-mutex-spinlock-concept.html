Semaphore vs Mutex vs Spinlock

Three tools for coordinating threads. A mutex: one owner at a time, blocked threads sleep. A semaphore: a counter allowing up to N at once. A spinlock: like a mutex but the waiter busy-spins instead of sleeping — great for very short waits, wasteful for long ones.
