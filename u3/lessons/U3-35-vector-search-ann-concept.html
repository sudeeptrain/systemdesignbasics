Vector Search & ANN (HNSW)

Comparing a query against every vector (exact search) is too slow at billions of points. Approximate Nearest Neighbour algorithms like HNSW build a navigable graph you hop through — landing on very-close matches in a few jumps, trading a tiny bit of accuracy for huge speed.
