Warehouse / Lake / Lakehouse — Pantry vs Garage

A pantry holds food, labelled and shelved — tidy and instantly usable, but everything must be sorted before it goes in. A garage takes anything, dumped as-is — flexible, but it can become a junk pile. A well-organized garage gives you both: store anything, yet still find it fast.
