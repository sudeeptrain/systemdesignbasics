Denormalization — A Meal Kit

To cook dinner the tidy way, you fetch each ingredient from a different shop — no waste, but lots of trips. A meal kit packs everything for one recipe into a single box: open it and cook, no running around — at the cost of some duplicated, pre-portioned items.
