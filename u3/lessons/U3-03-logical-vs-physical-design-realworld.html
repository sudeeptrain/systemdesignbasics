Logical vs Physical — Recipe vs Kitchen Prep

A recipe says WHAT the dish is: ingredients and how they relate. The kitchen's prep says HOW it's actually made here: pan sizes, oven temps, which station does what. The recipe travels anywhere; the prep is tuned to your specific kitchen.
