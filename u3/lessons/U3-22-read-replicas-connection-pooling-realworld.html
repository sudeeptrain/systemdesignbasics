Connection Pooling — A Taxi Rank

Phoning for a brand-new taxi on every trip means waiting for one to be dispatched and drive over — wasteful. A taxi rank keeps a few cabs idling out front: you hop in a waiting one, and it returns to the rank when you're done. Same idea, far less waiting.
