Warehouse vs Lake vs Lakehouse

Three ways to store data for analytics. A warehouse holds clean, structured tables (schema-on-write). A lake holds raw files of any kind (schema-on-read). A lakehouse adds warehouse-like structure and reliability on top of cheap lake storage.
