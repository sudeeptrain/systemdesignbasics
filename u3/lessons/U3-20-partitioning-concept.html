Partitioning (Horizontal vs Vertical)

Partitioning splits one big table into smaller pieces. Horizontal partitioning slices it by rows (e.g., by date or region); vertical partitioning slices it by columns (hot columns in one piece, rarely-used ones in another). Queries then touch only the slice they need.
